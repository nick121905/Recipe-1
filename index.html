Chocolate Milkshake Receipe

By: Nicholas Narine

If you like chocolate, learn how to make it into a Milkshake!

Ingredients:

1.) 3/4 Cup of Milk

2.) 1/4 Cup of Chocolate-Flavored Syrup

3.) 3 Scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream


Steps:
  1. Place Milk & Syrup In Blender
  2. Cover Blender & Blend On High Speed For 2 Minutes
  3. Add Ice Cream & Blend Again On Low Speed Until It Looks Smooth
  4. Pour Into Glass & Enjoy!
